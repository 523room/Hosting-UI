ACCESS RULE — ONE CONSISTENT RULE

403 Forbidden

You are authenticated, but the route/action is not mapped to your role — deny-by-default permission failure.

404 Not Found

The resource exists but is outside your remit or another FI's tenant. Existence is never disclosed — we never return 403 for out-of-remit data, because that would confirm it exists. Applied identically to FI cross-tenant and connector cross-access.
