Connectors / DSAs CreditBridge — qualify · enrich · route Financial Institutions
01 · WHAT THIS IS

An LSP marketplace

CreditBridge is not RBI-registered. It is a broker-like intermediary whose job is to manage leads and ensure no customer is left unsatisfied. It qualifies borrowers in 90 seconds, completes their document file, and routes them to the most eligible FI on a success-fee basis. It never lends, never touches funds, never makes the credit decision.

02 · THE PROBLEM

Three simultaneous failures

  • Connectors operate blind — paper workflows, no tracking, opaque commission. They churn.
  • Banks get incomplete files — 60–70% of FI re-work is document-related.
  • No neutral orchestrator — a ₹18–25,000 Cr commission pool with no matching, pricing, or accountability layer.
03 · THE SOLUTION

Physical origination, digital fulfilment

  • Connectors get a live pipeline, transparent split, payout in 48h.
  • FIs get ≥80%-complete, bureau-banded leads via sealed weighted bidding under SLA.
  • Borrowers get a 90-second answer and multi-lender access over WhatsApp.
