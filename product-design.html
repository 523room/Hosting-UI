The credit distribution
marketplace, made visible.

An RBI-registered Lending Service Provider under the Digital Lending Directions, 2025. CreditBridge sources credit leads through a physical connector network, qualifies them in real time, and routes them to the most eligible financial institution on a success-fee basis — across four product surfaces.
